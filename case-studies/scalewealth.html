Executive Summary

Scalewealth Properties operates high-end residential listings across Lagos, Nigeria. As their partner agent network scaled past 15+ agencies, managing buyers, deals, and commission splits manually via spreadsheets and chat logs became highly inefficient, resulting in lost leads and payout disputes.

Under the OLADEVX banner, we architected a bespoke real estate CRM and sales pipeline platform. The system visualizes the agency's sales funnel, automates marketing campaigns, coordinates referral agent commission structures, and generates monthly performance analytics.

The Challenge

The management firm suffered from three core operational bottlenecks:

  • Lead Leakage: Inbound inquiries from web portals, social media, and direct calls were unassigned or forgotten, leading to a 40% loss in prospective buyers.
  • Opaque Referral Commissions: Real estate deals in Nigeria involve multiple external agents. Calculating multi-tier commission splits manually led to payouts delays and disputes.
  • Fragmented Listing Pipeline: Agents had no central database to view listings, log buyer requirements (e.g., Waterfront Penthouses), or progress deals through pipeline stages.

The Solution

We developed a bespoke three-layered solution:

Leads Pipeline CRM

Designed an interactive, drag-and-drop pipeline board using React and framer-motion. Agents can easily transition hot leads from intake to inspection, offer, and final settlement with zero friction.

Referral & Commission Ledger

Built a secure ledger that automates payout splits. The system tracks referring agents, records commission percentages, notifies coordinators on successful closings, and handles payout triggers via bank API integrations.

White-Label Agency Branding

Implemented full white-label configurations. Real estate brokerages can customize domains, upload brand assets, and tailor dashboard themes, creating a fully branded customer portal in under 5 minutes.

Business Impact

Within 90 days of deployment, Scalewealth Properties recorded exceptional results:

  • 75% Lead Conversion: Bypassed lost inquiries, increasing conversion from 40% to 75% in the first 90 days of launch.
  • ₦150M+ Deals Managed: Reconciled and monitored pipeline deal volume across multiple high-end residential listings.
  • Zero Payout Disputes: Automated commission ledger splits eliminated payout delays and friction between brokers and external partners.
