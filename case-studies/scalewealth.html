Executive Summary

Scalewealth Properties manages a diverse portfolio of commercial and residential assets across Lagos, Nigeria. As they scaled past 150 units, managing operations via spreadsheet ledgers, paper receipts, and uncentralized text messages became unsustainable.

Under the OLADEVX banner, we architected and delivered a multi-tenant cloud dashboard. The platform fully automates monthly invoicing, integrates instant virtual bank account collections, reconciles payments, and logs maintenance tickets, saving hundreds of administrative hours.

The Challenge

The management firm suffered from three core operational bottlenecks:

  • Billing Drifts: Manually generating PDF rent statements and invoices for hundreds of tenants on different billing dates led to delays, calculations errors, and slow collections.
  • Manual Payment Auditing: Bank transfers are the dominant rent payment method in Nigeria. Accounting staff had to manually match screenshots of bank receipts against bank statements—a process vulnerable to fraud and human error.
  • Maintenance Tracking: Tenant complaints about plumbing, electrical, or structural issues were scattered across WhatsApp logs, leading to long delays and frustrated tenants.

The Solution

We developed a bespoke three-layered solution:

Automated Billing Engine

Developed a Node/TypeScript micro-billing service running on scheduled serverless Cron triggers. It automatically checks database leases daily, generates localized invoice files, compiles digital PDFs, and mails tenant alerts without manual staff inputs.

Payment Reconciliation Hooks

Integrated payment provider webhooks (Paystack) to allocate dedicated virtual bank account numbers to each tenant. When a tenant transfers rent to their assigned account, the webhook fires, the system matches the transaction code, updates the tenant ledger, sends an receipt, and alerts management instantly.

Maintenance Ticketing System

Built a tenant service portal allowing uploads of image logs, priority configurations (Low/Medium/High), and automated contractor assignment rules based on local service directories, resolving issues faster.

Business Impact

Within 90 days of deployment, Scalewealth Properties recorded exceptional results:

  • Saved Billing Hours: Automated billing saved the accounting team **32 working hours** per month.
  • Zero Reconciliation Errors: Direct virtual account bank transfers bypassed receipt matching audits, reducing manual ledger errors to absolute zero.
  • Happier Tenants: Issue routing timers dropped from an average of 5 days to **under 24 hours** because local handymen were alerted instantly via SMS when a high-priority ticket was opened.
