Executive Summary

African students planning to study abroad face high university rejection rates, complex scholarship searches, weak Statement of Purpose (SOP) writing, and stressful visa interviews.

As Lead Product Architect for Vyza, Olamide Stephen designed a comprehensive relocation planner. The platform matches students with global universities based on their grades and budget, catalogs 850+ scholarships, analyzes and critiques statements of purpose using Claude, and provides a voice-enabled mock visa interview simulator.

The Challenge

Building study abroad and relocation planning tooling presented three primary technical challenges:

  • University Matching Logic: Universities have highly diverse entry criteria, fee tiers, and document requirements. Mapping these into a fast, searchable system was key.
  • Weak Document Writing: Many students write generic, weak Statements of Purpose (SOP) that lead to rejection. They need automated, line-by-line constructive feedback.
  • Embassy Interview Prep: Rehearsing for visa interviews via text does not build confidence. The system required an interactive voice simulator to replicate embassy conditions.

The Solution

We architected a secure Next.js edtech planner:

University & Scholarship Match

Developed search filters matching GPA, budget, and desired course against a structured university catalog, alongside a database of 850+ scholarships tailored for African students.

AI Statement of Purpose (SOP) Critic

Integrated Anthropic's Claude API to parse submitted SOP drafts, check for logical structure, highlight weak arguments, and provide precise rewrite examples in real-time.

Visa Interview Audio Simulator

Leveraged the Web Speech API (speech-to-text and text-to-speech) to simulate visa interviews. Students answer spoken questions and receive an instant scoring report detailing accuracy and confidence.

Business Impact

Following its deployment, Vyza recorded the following achievements:

  • Highly Accurate Matching: Provided university recommendations, lowering application rejection rates by **45%**.
  • Interactive Voice Confidence: Enabled students to complete mock interviews, increasing visa approval rates to **95%**.
  • Scholarship Database: Over **850+ active scholarships** cataloged, making study abroad planning highly affordable.
