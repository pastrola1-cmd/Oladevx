Church Management SaaS

EkklesiaX Platform Architecture

Architected and built a modern church management SaaS enabling congregations to manage membership, automate digital giving via Paystack, and coordinate outreach.

Client / Organization Modern Ministries & Assemblies
Role Lead Systems Developer
Tech Stack Next.js, Supabase, Paystack API, WhatsApp API, Resend
Deployment Year 2026

The Business Problem

Traditional churches and modern ministries often struggle to track growth, keep databases updated, and maintain direct contact with members. Administrative teams spend significant resources handling cash tithes/offerings manually, which raises concerns about security and transparency.

Manual messaging networks lead to delayed notifications and lower community engagement across congregational cells.

The Solution: EkklesiaX

We designed and deployed EkklesiaX, a modular church management SaaS system. It integrates member profiles, donation ledgers, and automated notification triggers.

Using Paystack, congregations can process card and bank payments securely. The WhatsApp API integration automatically sends thank-you notes, announcements, and prayer requests directly to members' phones.

Technical Architecture

Paystack Rails & Messaging Loops

  • Paystack Webhooks: Secure webhooks auto-reconciling online collections into parish bank accounts.
  • WhatsApp API Integration: Automated triggers dispatching welcoming or birthday announcements to members.
  • Next.js Frontend: Client app with responsive controls for parish administrative staff.
  • Supabase Backend: Structured PostgreSQL database modeling congregations, groups, and financial logs.
