Executive Summary

Crown International Academies manages learning programs for over 1,200 students across multiple class segments. Their legacy operations relied on physical files, standalone paper grade books, and bank deposit teller slips. This led to lost records, errors in terminal averages, and delayed school fee reconciliations.

Under the OLADEVVX banner, we architected and deployed SchoolOS—a lightweight, mobile-responsive Web App. It consolidates student registration databases, automates report card math calculations, integrates digital payment APIs for fee collections, and publishes notifications directly to parents' mobile portals.

The Challenge

The institution's administrative team struggled with three core areas:

  • Enrollment Clashes: Hand-writing student profiles and printing ID details at the beginning of each academic session led to duplicate entries and registry discrepancies.
  • Calculation Slips: Teachers manually tallied exam marks and mid-term grades on paper ledger sheets. This resulted in math calculation mistakes on report cards and delayed distribution to parents.
  • Inefficient Collections: School fees collection involved tracking cash envelopes or verifying printed bank teller receipts. This made tracking defaults extremely difficult.

The Solution

We built and deployed SchoolOS, centered around three main functional pillars:

Automated Grading Engine

Built a calculation module inside the Python API that automatically processes test scores and exams, generates class averages, ranks class lists, and designs PDF report cards. This eliminated all manual spreadsheet calculations and grading errors.

Secure Tuition Portals

Integrated Flutterwave and Paystack payment widgets. Parents can check outstanding fees and pay securely via debit card, USSD, or direct bank transfer from their mobile phones. Transactions are automatically logged and receipts generated.

Parent Notification Publisher

Designed a background notification service that pushes automatic SMS and system updates to parents whenever report cards are finalized, fee invoices are issued, or school announcements are posted.

Business Impact

The school recorded positive feedback and improved operations within the first school term:

  • Eliminated Grading Errors: The grade calculation engine achieved **100% mathematical accuracy**, eliminating parent grade disputes completely.
  • Higher Collection Rates: Due to mobile payment portals, outstanding fee recovery rose by **30% in the first 30 days**.
  • Faster Turnarounds: Generating 1,200 terminal report cards dropped from **7 days of admin calculations to a single-click database operation**.
