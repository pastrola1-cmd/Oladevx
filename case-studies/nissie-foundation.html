Nonprofit Platform

Nissie Foundation Architecture

Architected and built a modern portal for the Nissie Idea Care Foundation to coordinate donation campaigns, process local payments, and manage volunteers.

Client / Organization Nissie Idea Care Foundation
Role Lead Systems Architect
Tech Stack Next.js 15, Supabase Database, Paystack API, Monnify
Deployment Year 2026

The Business Problem

Nonprofit foundations require secure, transparent channels to manage local and international donor relationships, process charity donations, and run volunteer onboarding campaigns. Existing systems are often complex, lack local payment integrations (like Monnify/Paystack), and charge high transaction percentages.

Without automated receipt generation and transparent spending boards, maintaining donor trust is difficult.

The Solution: Nissie Foundation Platform

Under the OLADEVVX banner, we engineered a custom nonprofit platform using Next.js 15. The system allows the foundation to publish campaign goals, accept donations securely via integrated local rails, and manage volunteer applications in a single unified system.

By combining Paystack and Monnify, transaction fees are minimized while offering bank transfer and card options.

Technical Architecture

Nonprofit Payment Integrations

  • Next.js 15 & React Server Components: Secure and fast page load times for campaigns.
  • Monnify & Paystack APIs: Flexible donation processing supporting local bank transfers and international cards.
  • Supabase Backend: Relational database securely mapping volunteers, donors, and transaction logs.
  • Automated PDF Receipting: Backend pipeline generating printable donation certificates upon webhook clearance.
