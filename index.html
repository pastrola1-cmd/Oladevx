The Challenge

Property management firms across Nigeria handle tenant billing, repair logs, and rent collections manually via Excel. This caused delayed payment alerts, unlogged tenant complaints, and accounting discrepancies.

The Solution

Architected a multi-tenant cloud-hosted platform enabling automated monthly PDF invoice delivery, instant bank transfer collection reconciliation, and repair ticket escalations. Built on a serverless microservice database.
